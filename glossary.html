Participatory Design
chapter 2

Persona
chapter 6
"User personas are [anonymized] archetypical users whose goals and characteristics represent the needs of a larger group of users." User personas are modeled after a particular person, but also incorporate fictional aspects that you use to create ‘ideal personas’ to compare and contrast with the ‘actual personas’. You will present a persona in a one to two-page document that describes their behavior patterns, goals, skills, attitudes, and background information, as well as the environment in which they operate.
